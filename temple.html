About the Sikh Temple

The Sikh temple, or Gurudwara, is a sacred place where Sikhs gather to worship, pray, and engage in spiritual learning. The word "Gurudwara" means the doorway to the Guru, a place where one can seek guidance and enlightenment.

Our Services

Daily Prayer Services

Join us for daily prayers and Gurbani recitations in the presence of the Guru Granth Sahib Ji.

Langar - Community Kitchen

The Langar is a free community kitchen that serves meals to all, irrespective of religion, caste, or background.

Educational Programs

We offer spiritual learning and educational programs to help individuals grow in their faith.
