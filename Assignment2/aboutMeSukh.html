Sukhpreet Singh

Hello, Hi, I'm Sukhpreet Singh, a cybersecurity student at Saskatchewan Polytechnic with a background in web development. Originally from India, I hold a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and a Postgraduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA) from Panjab University.

I'm excited to be here, expanding my skills, embracing new challenges, and connecting with like-minded individuals in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity. Looking forward to the journey ahead!
